# Property placeholder photos — credits & license

property or unit has no uploaded `photo_url`. Self-hosted (no third-party hotlinking)
to avoid the flakiness/rate-limiting of placeholder services.

All sourced from **Pexels** under the [Pexels License](https://www.pexels.com/license/)
(free to use, no attribution required; attribution recorded here as good practice).
Each was center-cropped to 16:9 and compressed to ~1200×675, <150 KB.
